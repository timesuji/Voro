Understand Influence

A Voronoi diagram shows you the nearest neighbor to every point - so you'll know the nearest coffee shop to every intersection, the nearest gas station to any point on the highway, or the nearest restaurant to the candidate locations for an event.

Use Google Maps

Enhance your analysis with the features of Google Maps. View traffic, switch from map to satellite view, see photos, look at the local weather - anything to help you understand your data.

View Any Dimension

Would you like to view average GPV by store? You can do that. How about superimposing that with the average population density of the area? You can do that too.
